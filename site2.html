Welcome To IJPREMS
International Journal of Progressive Research in Engineering Management and Science (IJPREMS) is a fully refereed peer-reviewed, open-access, multidisciplinary journal which publishes Original Research Article, Review Article, Case Reports, conference proceedings, from all the fields of Engineering, Management, Science and Technology. IJPREMS provides platform to the researchers, scholars, scientist and academician to share their knowledge and research ideas online. IJPREMS enables its readers to access the published articles free of cost. Papers published on IJPREMS are also available on all the indexing services adopted by the journal.
